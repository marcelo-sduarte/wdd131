How did it all begin?

A Duarte Doceria was born from our passion for being in the kitchen, discovering flavors, testing recipes, and perfecting them. With the support and encouragement of our family and friends, this flavorful business came to life. We are constantly striving to do our best to serve you, and your feedback and opinions are taken very seriously. Our mission is to bring a homemade yet contemporary taste to your table, making sweet memories last forever
